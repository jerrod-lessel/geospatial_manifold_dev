About Geospatial Manifold

Welcome to Geospatial Manifold! Your tool for exploring hazard, health, and resilience data across California. Click anywhere on the map to get a full location report with charts, environmental data, and geological risk info.

Data comes from trusted sources including CAL FIRE, OEHHA, the California Geological Survey, USGS, and FEMA.

Created by Jerrod Lessel, a geospatial analyst at Caltrans on California's Central Coast.
